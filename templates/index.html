SHPE is the nations largest association dedicated to fostering Hispanic leadership in the STEM field.

Leadership Development

SHPE provides resources and opportunities for professional development and leadership training for its members, including workshops, conferences, and networking events.

Community Service

SHPE inspires its members to take part in community outreach programs that focus on increasing awareness and participation in STEM fields among underrepresented groups.

STEM Education

SHPE promotes and supports STEM education programs and initiatives, such as mentoring and scholarship programs, to increase the participation and success of Hispanic students in STEM fields.

Career Advancement

SHPE provides resources and opportunities to help its members advance in their professional careers, such as mentorship programs, career fairs, and job placement services.
