Empowering the Hispanic Community in STEM

Our focus is on uplifting and supporting the Hispanic community in STEM by increasing awareness, providing access to resources, promoting professional development, and fostering a sense of community among members.

Read our story

Our Founding

Our founders recognized the importance of representation and support for Hispanic students within the STEM field and decided to establish this chapter club as a resource for their peers. The club has grown and evolved since its inception, becoming a leading organization in the field. We are proud of the impact we have made and continue to make in the STEM field, and specifically in the Hispanic community at Miami Dade College.

Growth & Beyond

Our mission at MDC SHPE is to empower and support our members in reaching their full potential through professional development, networking opportunities, and community building. Join our community and discover the endless possibilities that come with growing and expanding your horizons.
